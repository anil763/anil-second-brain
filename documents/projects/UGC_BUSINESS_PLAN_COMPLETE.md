## Cold Email Strategy

### Domain Warmup Phase (Weeks 1–2)
- **Volume:** 10–20 emails/day
- **Focus:** List validation, building sender reputation
- **Expect:** Low response (normal during warmup)
- **Tools:** GoHighLevel warmup sequence

### Ramp-Up Phase (Weeks 3–4)
- **Volume:** 30–50 emails/day
- **Focus:** Real outreach, personalized pitches
- **Expect:** 3–5% response rate
- **Tools:** Full sequence in GoHighLevel with tracking

### Scale Phase (Week 5+)
- **Volume:** 50–75 emails/day (6-hour time block)
- **Focus:** Consistent cold outreach + proposal follow-ups
- **Expect:** 5–8% response rate (3–6 positive replies/day)
- **Tools:** GoHighLevel automation + manual responses

### Email Formula (Proven to Work)

**Subject Line:** Personalized, curiosity-based (not sales-y)
- ✓ "quick thought about [Company Name]'s TikTok strategy"
- ✓ "GenX decision makers respond to [Product] differently"
- ✓ "I created something for [Company Type] like yours"

**Body:**
1. **Personalization:** Reference their company, recent campaign, market position (2–3 lines)
2. **Your Angle:** Why you specifically can help (GenX authenticity, no product dependency)
3. **Proof:** 1–2 line portfolio reference (Ship4WD, dealerships, fintech)
4. **CTA:** Single, clear ask ("10-min call Friday?" or "Quick questions: 1. ... 2. ...")
5. **Signature:** Your name, title, portfolio link

**Length:** 75–150 words (short, scannable)

**Tone:** Peer-to-peer, not salesy. You're offering value, not asking for a job.

## Critical Success Factors

### 1. Domain Warmup (Weeks 1–2)
- This isn't optional. Skip it = your emails hit spam.
- Follow GoHighLevel's recommended warmup sequence.
- Don't go full-volume until warmup is complete.

### 2. Personalization Over Volume
- 20 personalized emails > 100 generic emails
- Reference their company, campaign, or market position
- Show you've done research (LinkedIn, website, recent news)

### 3. Speed to Proposal
- From positive reply → discovery call within 24 hours
- From call → proposal within 48 hours
- Speed wins deals when there are competing creators

### 4. Portfolio Credibility
- You need 3–5 solid examples linked in email signature
- Backstage work counts (embed video, show quality)
- Ship4WD + dealership work = strong proof for B2B

### 5. Consistent Execution
- Cold email is not a one-time thing
- You must send 20–75 emails EVERY WEEK for 90 days
- Consistency beats perfection. Done > perfect.
