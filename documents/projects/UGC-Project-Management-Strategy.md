## 🎬 VIDEO CONCEPTS - Shoot These First

### Concept 1: "How I Organize My Entire Business in [TOOL]"
**Platform:** YouTube (5-8 min) + TikTok teaser (60s)
**Hook:** "This is how I manage 3 income streams in one dashboard..."
**Content:**
- Quick tour of your workspace
- Show: numerology clients → UGC submissions → daily content calendar → business metrics
- Explain your workflow for each
- Show the automation/automations that save you time
- CTA: "This is why I switched to [TOOL]"

**Why brands love it:** Authentic, real-world usage, aspirational, relatable

---

### Concept 2: "I Switched From [Competitor] to [TOOL] - Here's Why"
**Platform:** YouTube (4-6 min) + TikTok (90s)
**Hook:** "I used Asana for 2 years. Then I tried ClickUp..."
**Content:**
- What you liked about old tool
- What frustrated you
- First impression of new tool
- Top 3 features that changed your workflow
- Honest pricing/value comparison
- Would you switch? (Yes, but...)

**Why brands love it:** Comparison content drives conversions, honest reviews build trust

---

### Concept 3: "The Automation That Saved Me 10 Hours/Week"
**Platform:** YouTube (3-5 min) + TikTok (60s)
**Hook:** "This one automation cut my admin time in half..."
**Content:**
- Show the problem (manual task you were doing)
- Show the [TOOL] setup (screen recording)
- Demo the automation in action
- Show the time saved / impact on your business
- How you're using those 10 hours now (reinvestment in business)

**Why brands love it:** Specific, valuable, shows ROI, shareable

---

### Concept 4: "Setup Walkthrough - [Business Type] Operating System"
**Platform:** YouTube (6-10 min)
**Hook:** "Here's exactly how I set up my business operating system in [TOOL]..."
**Content:**
- Workspace overview
- Folder/section structure
- Templates you created
- Automations running in background
- Integration with other tools you use
- Monthly review process
- Lessons learned from setup

**Why brands love it:** In-depth, educational, long-form keeps viewers engaged, perfect for brand features

## 🔥 Pro Tips

1. **Use the tools for real** — Actually use ClickUp, Notion, etc. for your business. Your authenticity is your edge.

2. **Document as you go** — Record screen captures, behind-the-scenes, your reactions. This becomes content.

3. **Engage with the tools on social** — Comment on official posts, use their hashtags. Brands notice engaged creators.

4. **Be specific in pitches** — "I help GenX entrepreneurs" not "I make content." Specificity converts partnerships.

5. **Follow up 1 week after applying** — Most brands don't read first emails. Persistence wins.

6. **Track everything** — Spreadsheet with: company, date applied, contact name, status, follow-up date.
