### 📝 Script Types That Work Best

**High Conversion Formats:**

1. **The Hook + Testimonial**
   > "I was skeptical about [product] until..." → 3 benefits → CTA

2. **Problem Agitation Solution (PAS)**
   > "[Problem statement] → [Make it worse] → [Product as hero]"

3. **Day in My Life Integration**
   > "Here's how I use [product] in my actual workflow..."

4. **The Comparison**
   > "I tried [alternatives] but [product] actually works because..."

5. **The Genuine Review**
   > "Okay, honest thoughts on [product] after 2 weeks..."

**Template 2: Tech/App Specific**

>
> I actually [use similar products / am familiar with this space], so I can speak authentically about it.
>

> 1. Who's your target audience (age, demographic)?
> 2. What angle works best — testimonial, problem/solution, review?
> 3. Any existing ads I can reference for tone/style?
>
> Looking forward to learning more!"

### 🎯 Questions to Ask Every Client

**Essential:**
1. What's the #1 goal for this content?
2. Who is your target customer (age, pain points)?
3. Do you have scripts or should I develop them?
4. Any brand guidelines or tone requirements?
5. What's your timeline?

**Helpful:**
6. What platforms will this run on?
7. Have you done UGC before? What worked/didn't?
8. Any competitor ads I should see?
9. Is this a test or ongoing campaign?

### 💼 Positioning as Expert

**Don't say:**
> "I can do whatever you need!"

**Do say:**
> "Based on what you've shared, I'd recommend starting with a hook-style testimonial — those tend to convert best for [product type]. I've seen brands like yours get great results with the problem/solution angle. Happy to share some examples."

### 📈 Upsell Templates
