## ⚠️ COMMON PITFALLS TO AVOID

### ❌ DON'T:
1. **Wait for perfect** — Launch ugly, iterate fast
2. **Film all at once** — Batch film, but don't burn out
3. **Skip the email list** — Start building NOW (it's your biggest asset)
4. **Be vague on pricing** — Clear tiers, clear benefits
5. **Soft launch** — Create urgency, or people will wait forever
6. **Ignore comments** — Engagement = trust = sales
7. **Overcomplicate** — 5 modules is enough to start
8. **Ghost your first members** — Over-deliver on onboarding
9. **Price too low** — $97 minimum, you're not selling courses, you're selling transformation
10. **Do it alone** — Build in public, ask for feedback

### ✅ DO:
1. **Launch with founding members** — Get 5-10 people in, learn fast
2. **Iterate based on feedback** — Let members shape the community
3. **Show up consistently** — Daily posts during launch week
4. **Personal outreach** — DMs convert better than posts
5. **Create urgency** — Deadlines, limited spots, bonuses
6. **Celebrate wins** — Share member progress publicly (with permission)
7. **Build the plane while flying** — Content doesn't have to be perfect
8. **Trust your story** — Your journey IS the value
