## Critical Success Factors

**1. Personal Touch**
- Email each person individually (not mass campaigns)
- Call anyone who asks
- Remember their names

**2. Social Proof**
- Get 1-2 early wins (case studies)
- Ask members to share in their networks
- Screenshot testimonials

**3. Community Vibe**
- Make members feel special (you chose them)
- Facilitate connections (member-to-member)
- Celebrate wins (public shoutouts)

**4. Deliver on Promise**
- Module 1 MUST hit by week 1
- Calls MUST be on time
- Community MUST feel active

**5. Permission to Leave**
- Be clear: 30-day refund, month-to-month billing
- This removes objection ("What if it sucks?")
- Paradoxically, makes more people stay
