## Risks & Contingencies

### Risk 1: Vault Launch Flops (Low warm list)
- **Contingency:** Extend pre-launch, build list from numerology community (1K+ TikTok followers)
- **Fallback:** Start at 20 members, grow organically, don't rely on $2.5K baseline until April

### Risk 2: UGC Scaling Hits Apollo Credit Limits
- **Contingency:** Upgrade Apollo plan ($299/month for 10K credits) or reduce outreach to 60/day
- **Fallback:** Focus on higher-quality outreach (fewer companies, better conversion)

### Risk 3: Readings Decline During Vault Focus
- **Contingency:** Build SMS nurture agent immediately (passive income from warm list)
- **Fallback:** Increase reading price to $197 to maintain revenue at lower volume

### Risk 4: NEXUS CODE Fails to Launch on Time
- **Contingency:** Launch May 15 instead of May 1, build wait list aggressively March-April
- **Fallback:** Delay to June 1, compensate with higher UGC/Vault targets
