# Anil's Origin Story

## Early Childhood (Pre-1986)

Born February 16, 1975 in London, England. Happy beginning — curiosity-driven. I loved taking apart toys to understand how they worked, then rebuilding them. But early memories are filled with yelling, criticism, and toxic messaging.

**Defining Wounds:**
- Mother saying "I wish one of you would die"
- Constant messaging: "You're useless. You'll never amount to anything."
- Complete emotional abandonment once my sisters were born
- Learned early: I am not worthy of love or attention

---

## The London Years (1986-1997)

**Age 11: Moved to USA**

Disoriented. Lonely. Bullied for accent, clothing, being "different." Twice attempted suicide. Once planned running away and changing identity — read books on how to do it.

**Core Belief Formed:**
*I am unlovable. The world is dangerous. I must numb the pain.*

---

## The Lost Decade (1997-2006)

**1997: Met my twin flame.** Age gap, lied about my age, she found out. Mother said NO. Relationship ended.

**2000: Another soulmate connection.** Mother called her mother, ended it again.

**1997-2006: Numbing Spiral**
- Heavy drinking and drugs
- Stripping clubs just to pay girls to sit and talk (nothing physical)
- Running from love whenever it appeared
- Relationship patterns:
  1. Woman says "I love you" → I run
  2. Woman wants commitment/closeness → I run
  3. Woman talks marriage → I run

**The Core Fear:**
*If I let anyone love me, they will leave me. They will be taken from me. The pain will be unbearable. So I won't allow it. I won't allow myself to feel.*

**June 25, 2006: Marriage**
- Why? "I won't meet anyone else. Let's get it over with."
- Red flags ignored. Heart completely closed.
- Completely erased the memory of the 1997 relationship.

## The Reckoning Years (2006-2019)

**First Pancreatitis Attack (2006):**
- Diagnosed Type 2 Diabetes

- Stopped drinking to heal (positive action)

**2019: Lost hearing in right ear**

**Marriage Reality:**
