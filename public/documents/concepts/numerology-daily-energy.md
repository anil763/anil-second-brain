# Numerology Daily Energy Strategy

## Definition
Daily numerology readings paired with actionable guidance, posted across multiple platforms to build audience and spiritual authority.

---

## The Model

### 1. Daily Energy Reading (Every Morning)
**Process:**
- Calculate the day's primary + secondary numerology energy
- Interpret meaning (emotional tone, relationship dynamic, action theme)
- Create hook + explanation + practical step + CTA

**Example (Feb 3, 2026: 6 & 3 Energy):**
- **Primary:** 6 (responsibility, care, relationships, emotional balance)
- **Secondary:** 3 (communication, creativity, expression)
- **Combined Theme:** Heart-led communication. Words carry extra weight today.

### 2. Content Script Structure

**[HOOK – 3-5 seconds]**
- Relatable statement that pulls viewer in immediately
- Sets emotional tone
- Example: *"Be careful with your words today… they carry extra weight."*

**[BODY – 45-60 seconds]**
- Explain primary energy (what it means)
- Explain secondary energy (what it adds)
- Show how they work together
- Describe emotional/behavioral experience
- Give permission or validation

**[PRACTICAL STEP – 15 seconds]**
- One specific action for today
- Must be doable in under 5 minutes
- Creates engagement + trust
- Example: *"Have one honest, caring conversation today"*

**[CALL TO ACTION – 5-7 seconds]**
- Engagement prompt (comments)
- Personalization hook (ask for life path number)
- Pro tip (filming technique/energy hack)

### 3. Distribution

**Platforms:**
- TikTok (short form, trending audio)
- Instagram (Reels, Stories, Feed post with caption)
- YouTube (short form, discoverable)
- Facebook (Longer form, community-focused)

**Frequency:** Daily (consistency = algorithm boost + routine for audience)

## Filming & Production

### Pro Tips (From Anil's Experience)

**Tone & Energy:**
- Warm, gentle eye contact for 6 & 3 energies
- Intense, determined look for 8 & 1 energies
- Vulnerable, reflective for 7 & 9 energies
- Match the energy in your face/body

**Hook Delivery:**
- Lead with the hook (first 3 seconds)
- Make it relatable + specific to the energy
- Avoid generic "Today's energy is..." openers
- Use conversational language

**Filming Setup:**
- Natural lighting (window preferred)
- Simple background (clean, consistent)
- Consistent time of day (same look/energy)
- Steady camera or tripod (not shaky)
