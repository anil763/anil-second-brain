# UGC Content Playbook

## Definition
User-Generated Content strategy for GenX audience. Create authentic, relatable video testimonials for brands (apps, fintech, tech). Get paid $100-500 per video submission.

---

## The UGC Model

### Who You Are
- GenX creator targeting GenX audience
- Authentic voice, real life experience
- Focus areas: Apps, Financial services, Tech products
- Casual, conversational, relatable

### Who You Work With
- Brands seeking authentic testimonials
- Platforms: Bento Backstage (20 emails/day outreach)
- Low-barrier-to-entry brand requests
- Volume-based (more submissions = more acceptances)

### How It Works

1. **Find Brand Opportunity** (via Bento Backstage)
   - Brand posts UGC brief
   - Includes: product, script guidelines, audience
   - Deadline: usually 3-7 days

2. **Create Video** (15 min - 1 hour)
   - Follow brief loosely (authenticity > perfection)
   - GenX angle: relatable life situations
   - App usage demo: Show, don't just tell
   - Testimonial format: "I tried X, here's why it's great"

3. **Submit**
   - Upload via Bento or brand platform
   - Include short description
   - Wait for approval

4. **Get Paid**
   - Typical: $100-500 per accepted video
   - Payment in 30-45 days (sometimes sooner)
   - Keep rights to repurpose on personal channel

## GenX Content Angle

### Why GenX?
- Often overlooked by brands (focus on millennial/Gen Z)
- Highly loyal, actual purchasing power
- Skeptical of corporate BS (authenticity wins)
- Love practical, real-life use cases
- Nostalgic but forward-thinking

### Your Positioning
- "The GenX perspective on X"
- Real life: Balancing work, family, finances, tech
- Honest: What actually works, not marketing fluff
- Relatable: "Here's how this fits my life"

### Content Angles for GenX

**Financial Apps:**
- "How I'm finally getting my finances together after 20 years of chaos"
- "Why this app is better than bugging my accountant"

- "The simplicity is what gets me"

## Video Script Template (GenX Flavor)

### [HOOK – 3-5 seconds]
Relatable problem statement
- "So I've been trying to manage my [side business/finances/schedule]..."
- "My kids asked me why I'm still using [outdated solution]..."
- "I'm not a tech person, but this one actually clicked..."

### [PROBLEM – 10 seconds]
Why the old way wasn't working
- Too complicated
- Too time-consuming
- Doesn't sync with how I actually work
- Felt like homework

### [DISCOVERY – 15 seconds]
How you found this app/tool
- Friend recommended
- Saw it on [platform]
- Finally decided to try something new
- Skeptical at first (GenX = healthy doubt)

### [DEMO – 30-45 seconds]
Show it in action
- Open the app
- Show key features
- Use natural language ("What I love is...")
- Don't read from script (authenticity)

### [PAYOFF – 10 seconds]
Why it matters to you
- Saves me X hours/week
- Finally got my [thing] under control
- Actually enjoying using it
- Recommended to a friend

### [CTA – 5 seconds]
Call to action
- "Worth checking out if you're in the same boat"
- "Game changer for [your situation]"
- "Link in bio" or "Available on [platform]"

## Filming Best Practices

### Setup
- Natural lighting (phone flashlight bounced off ceiling works)
- Clean, clutter-free background (or slightly lived-in, not messy)
- Phone on tripod or propped up
- Minimal distractions

### Audio
- Quiet room (no background noise)
- Speak clearly, conversational pace
- Pause after statements (editing-friendly)
- No background music (brand adds it)

### Pacing
- Don't rush (feels desperate)
- Don't drawl (feels boring)
- Natural conversational speed
- Let emotions be genuine

### Authenticity (The Secret Sauce)
- You're not an actor, you're YOU
- Slight imperfections = more relatable
- Show real frustration with old way
- Genuine relief/joy with new solution
- If you mess up: do another take (or submit both)

## Pro Tips from the Field

1. **Quantity beats perfection** — Submit more good videos, not fewer perfect ones
2. **Follow the brief, but make it yours** — They want authenticity within guidelines
3. **Lead with benefit** — What does this app do for GenX specifically?
4. **Show, don't tell** — Demos work better than explanations
5. **Be the customer first** — Use the app for 24-48h before filming
6. **Network in UGC communities** — Collab with other creators, share tips
7. **Repurpose everything** — One video = multiple platforms = multiple paydays
