## How to Customize These Scripts

**Fill-in variables:**
- `[Tool]` = Your specific tool (ClickUp, Monday, Notion, etc.)
- `[Your business model]` = UGC, coaching, content, etc.
- `[Specific feature]` = Whatever you want to highlight
- `[Time saved/money made]` = Real numbers from your business

**Timing Tips:**
- 0:00-0:05 = Hook (must be attention-grabbing)
- 0:05-0:40 = Problem + Solution (body content)
- 0:40-1:00 = Result + CTA (close strong)

**Camera & Audio:**
- Film in landscape (vertical for TikTok/Reels)
- Use natural lighting (window is best)
- Speak conversationally (not scripted)
- Show your actual screen/tools (real > fake)
- Include your face (personal connection)

**Post-Production:**
- Cut out long pauses
- Use quick transitions
- Add captions (some viewers mute)
- Music in background (low volume, non-distracting)
