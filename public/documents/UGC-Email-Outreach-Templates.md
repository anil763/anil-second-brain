## Pro Tips for Success

1. **Personalization is key** → Use their name, mention your actual experience with the tool
2. **Lead with benefit to them** → "Your audience needs to hear about this" not "Can I make money?"
3. **Speed commitment** → "Can turn this around in 48 hours"
4. **Show you use it** → Mention specific features/results, not generic praise
5. **Make it easy to say yes** → Offer samples, ask simple next step
6. **Send from personal email** → anilgunjal@[yourdomain].com (professional, not spammy)
7. **Follow up after 1 week** → "Just checking in on the video opportunity"

## Fill-in-the-Blank Variables

Use these placeholders:
- `[Tool]` → ClickUp, Monday.com, Notion, etc.
- `[Competitor]` → What you switched from (e.g., Asana, Trello)
- `[Specific use case]` → What you use it for (e.g., managing UGC submissions, organizing numerology content)
- `[Timeframe]` → 2 weeks, 1 month, 3 months
- `[Specific metric]` → Time saved, revenue impact, efficiency gain
- `[Name]` → Partnership manager first name (research LinkedIn)
- `[Website link]` → anilcreates.com
- `[Portfolio link]` → Bento Backstage or portfolio samples
