# PRODUCTION NOTES

## Filming Best Practices

**Setup:**
- Clean, uncluttered background (home office, bookshelf, neutral)
- Natural light or ring light (soft, not harsh)
- Camera at eye level
- Minimal distractions

**Delivery:**
- Conversational, not scripted-sounding
- Direct to camera (eye contact with lens)
- Natural pace—slightly slower than feels normal
- Genuine energy—authenticity over polish

**Technical:**
- 1080p minimum (4K preferred)
- Good audio is more important than good video
- Lapel mic or quality shotgun mic
- Film in batches (4-5 videos per session)

## Publishing Strategy

**Priority Order:**
1. Launch Videos (1.1-1.5) — Publish first, form the foundation
2. Sales Videos (2.1-2.6) — Roll out during launch week
3. Evergreen Content (5.1-5.4) — Publish weekly for SEO/discovery
4. Educational Content (3.1-3.6) — Mix into ongoing content calendar
5. Community Engagement (4.1-4.4) — Ongoing, as community grows

**Platform Strategy:**
- **YouTube:** Long-form home, SEO plays, all videos
- **LinkedIn:** Business-focused content (1.3, 3.3, 3.4, 5.3, 5.4)
- **TikTok:** Hooks and key clips, under 60 seconds
- **Telegram:** All content, plus exclusive community updates
- **Instagram:** Reels from TikTok content, stories for engagement

## Repurposing Guide

Each script can generate:
- Full video (YouTube, Telegram)
- 60-second clip (TikTok, Reels)
- Quote graphic (Instagram, LinkedIn)
- Written post (LinkedIn article, blog)
- Email newsletter content
- Community discussion prompt
