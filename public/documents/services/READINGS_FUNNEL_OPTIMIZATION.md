## Phase 3: Conversion Mechanics (Week 2-3)

### Reduce Friction

**Current Booking Process:**
1. User clicks "Book a Reading"
2. Where do they go?
   - Stripe? (good for payments)
   - Calendly? (good for scheduling)
   - Email form? (requires back-and-forth)

**Recommendation:** 
- **Stripe** for payment + email collection
- **Calendly** (embedded) for scheduling
- One-page checkout flow (no distractions)

### Price Strategy

**Current (unknown):** ~$100 average

**Recommended 3-Tier:**
- **Quick Read**: $97 (15 min reading, written summary)
- **Full Chart**: $197 (30 min Zoom, deep dive, written chart)
- **Coaching Package**: $497 (Full Chart + 2 coaching calls, 30-day follow-up)

**Why this works:**
- $97 = impulse buy (many conversions)
- $197 = sweet spot (perceived value vs price)
- $497 = high-ticket (upsell to serious people)

**Math to $2K:**
- Option A: 20 × $97 = $1,940
- Option B: 8 × $197 + 2 × $497 = $2,570
- Option B is better (fewer bookings, higher revenue, better clients)

### Urgency + Scarcity

- "I take 3 readings/week to ensure quality"
- "Spots booked 2-3 weeks out"
- "Booking closes [Friday] to maintain my schedule"

This is TRUE if you say it (you control your capacity). Use it.
