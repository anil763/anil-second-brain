## 3. Pricing Strategy

### Founding Member Rate: $44/month

- **Why $44?** Master Number in numerology—the Master Healer and Builder. Use this in your messaging.
- **Why low?** Remove friction for men curious but not yet committed

### Future Pricing (After 20–25 members)
- Raise to $66/month or $77/month

### Optional Add-On: 1:1 Reading
- Offer discounted 1:1 reading at $77 (instead of $97)
- Cross-pollinate your reading business with the Vault
- Vault members also get quarterly group reading sessions as exclusive perk

## 4. What's Inside the Vault

Keep it lean. You have 1–2 hours per week. Maximum value, minimum time drain.

### Weekly Rhythm (1–2 hours/week total)

#### 1. The Weekly Drop (15–20 min to create)
Short video or audio posted to Skool community each week. This is your anchor content.

**Monthly Theme Rotation:**
- **Week 1 — Purpose & Direction** 
  - What does alignment actually look like for men? How do you know when you're off-path?

- **Week 2 — Emotional Mastery**
  - Patterns, triggers, conditioning. How to feel without drowning. How to process without shutting down.

- **Week 3 — Masculine Leadership**
  - What it means to lead yourself first. The difference between performing strength and embodying it.

- **Week 4 — Energy & Awareness**
  - How energy works in your life, relationships, and decisions. Practical awareness, not woo.

**Production Note:** These don't need to be polished. Sit down, talk to the camera or record audio, and share from experience. Raw and real is the brand.

#### 2. Monthly Live Call (60 min)
This is where the magic happens.

**Format:**
- **First 15 min:** Anil shares a theme or lesson for the month
- **Next 30 min:** Open discussion, hot seat, Q&A (men bring what's real)
- **Final 15 min:** Anil pulls energy reads, offers guidance, or shares insights

**Post-Call:** Record and add to Vault. Replay library becomes a growing asset.

#### 3. Community Thread (5 min/day, optional)
Drop a daily or every-other-day prompt in Skool community.

**Examples:**
- "What are you carrying today that you haven't told anyone?"
- "What would change if you stopped performing strength?"
- "When did you last feel truly alive?"

**Purpose:** Spark conversation and keep space alive without requiring constant moderation.
