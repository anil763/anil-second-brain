# Memory Bank Agent 🧠

**Status:** ✅ LIVE (Real-time)

**Purpose:** Automatically capture your thoughts and ideas from Discord #memory-bank channel and sync them to your Second Brain.

---

## How It Works

1. **You post in #memory-bank** - Any thought, idea, decision, or insight you have
2. **Agent listens in real-time** - Immediately pulls your message
3. **Auto-creates memory entry** - Adds to Second Brain with timestamps and categories
4. **Tags & organizes** - Categorizes as idea/insight/decision/blocker
5. **Links to documents** - Connects related thoughts to your existing documents

---

## What to Post

- **🔍 Insights:** "Noticed UGC brands respond better to Gen X language"
- **💡 Ideas:** "What if I created a numerology AI chatbot?"
- **📋 Decisions:** "Decided to focus on $2K/month readings target first"
- **🚫 Blockers:** "Wife keeps triggering me about financial risk"
- **❓ Questions:** "Should I pivot to coaching-focused content?"

---

## Output

- **Real-time entries** in Second Brain memory system
- **Auto-tagged** by category
- **Connected** to relevant documents
- **Searchable** via Second Brain search
- **Daily summary** of all thoughts captured
