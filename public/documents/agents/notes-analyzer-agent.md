# Notes Analyzer Agent 📝

**Status:** ✅ LIVE (Daily @ 9:00 AM EST)

**Purpose:** Analyze all your notes and generate actionable insights, patterns, and connections.

---

## How It Works

1. **You dump notes in #notes** - Any note, thought, observation, or snippet
2. **Agent collects daily** - At 9:00 AM EST, pulls all notes from the past 24 hours
3. **Deep analysis** - Identifies patterns, themes, and recurring ideas
4. **Finds insights** - Detects actionable items and blockers
5. **Creates connections** - Links notes to your documents and memory
6. **Posts summary** - Delivers analysis to #notes channel + Second Brain document

---

## What to Post

- **📚 Research notes:** Content ideas, competitor findings, market research
- **💭 Thinking:** Brain dumps, strategic musings, problem-solving
- **📊 Data:** Stats, metrics, conversion rates, revenue numbers
- **🎯 Planning:** Strategy ideas, product concepts, business pivots
- **✍️ Writing:** Draft copy, headlines, email sequences
- **🔗 Connections:** Links between ideas and documents

---

## Daily Analysis Includes

### 1. **Pattern Detection**
- What themes recurred in your notes?
- What ideas appeared multiple times?
- What problems showed up repeatedly?

### 2. **Actionable Insights**
- "Your notes show 3x focus on email vs. video content"
- "Recurring blocker: Perfectionism delaying execution"
- "Opportunity: Notes mention AI + Numerology 5 times"

### 3. **Blockers & Wins**
- What's stopping progress?
- What wins should you celebrate?
- What decisions need making?

### 4. **Next Steps**
- Prioritized actions based on your notes
- Quick wins you can do today
- Strategic decisions to make

### 5. **Document Connections**
- Links your notes to existing strategies
- Suggests updates to dashboards
- Identifies gaps in planning
